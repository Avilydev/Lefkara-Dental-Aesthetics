Skin Analysis & Facials

Your skin changes gradually. Sometimes, texture shifts or tone becomes uneven, and it may not always be clear why. At Lefkara Dental & Aesthetics, skin treatments begin with careful evaluation, so recommendations are guided by what your skin actually needs.

Why start with skin analysis?

A consultation at our practice in Phakalane includes VISIA Skin Analysis, which provides a detailed look beneath the surface. Pigmentation patterns and areas of congestion can be identified clearly. This ensures treatment is selected with purpose.

Starting with skin analysis can help you:

  • Recognise patterns such as congestion or pigmentation
  • Understand whether your current routine is helping
  • Avoid choosing treatments too quickly
  • Plan your next step with more confidence

What facials are available?

Once your skin has been assessed, the clinical team recommends a facial suited to your skin's current condition. Available treatments include:

  • Medi Facial for maintenance
  • Acu Cleanse for acne management
  • Brightening facials for uneven tone
  • Hydra Fusion for hydration support
  • LED phototherapy, including Dermalux LED sessions where appropriate

Each treatment is chosen according to what your skin requires at that moment.

What happens during treatment?

During your visit with a Dentist in Phakalane, your skin is cleansed thoroughly and treated according to the agreed plan. The focus remains on gradual improvement and maintaining skin integrity.

If your skin would benefit from deeper resurfacing, this can be discussed during your consultation and explored further within our skin peels & advanced skin rejuvenation treatments. Where facial movement or volume changes are also part of your concern, options available under anti-wrinkle treatments & injectable aesthetics may be considered following the assessment.

Ready to understand your skin better?

If your skin feels different, consulting our dentist in Phakalane is a good first step. A detailed assessment reveals what's beneath the surface before treatment. Contact Lefkara Dental & Aesthetics to schedule your visit and move toward clearer skin.
