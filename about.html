About Lefkara Dental & Aesthetics

Our Practice

Lefkara Dental & Aesthetics is a premium dental and facial aesthetics practice based in Mowana Park Mall, Phakalane, Gaborone, Botswana. The practice was established to bring dental science and aesthetic medicine together within one carefully designed clinical setting.

At Lefkara, we do not look at your teeth in isolation. How your teeth function and how you feel about your appearance both matter. When you visit, we take the time to assess what is happening and explain it clearly, so you understand the next step before moving forward.

The vision of the practice is to serve as a centre for holistic dental and aesthetic treatment in Botswana, recognised for clinical integrity and a wellness-focused approach to health.

Care at Lefkara is shaped by clear values. Clinical decisions are grounded in evidence. Treatment is delivered with transparency. Patients are encouraged to understand their options and participate in informed decision-making. The environment is designed to feel calm and reassuring from the first consultation onward.

Founder & Lead Clinician

Dr Bonolo Mannathoko

Principal Dentist & Aesthetic Practitioner

  • Doctor of Dental Surgery (UWI, 2009)
  • Master of Clinical Stomatology in Orthodontics (WMU, 2015)
  • Board Certified in Aesthetic Medicine (AAAM, 2024)
  • Modern Aesthetic Dentistry P.G Mastership (BARD, 2026)
  • Diploma in Restorative & Aesthetic Dentistry (RAA, ongoing)

Dr Bonolo Mannathoko is a dentist and aesthetic practitioner with a focused interest in preventive dentistry, cosmetic dentistry, orthodontics, and facial aesthetics. Her clinical philosophy centres on minimally invasive treatment planning that supports both function and natural appearance.

With further training in both dentistry and aesthetic medicine, she developed a deeper understanding of how oral health can affect facial balance. That perspective influences how she evaluates each case and plans treatment.

She continues to study and refine her skills and remains involved in teaching and community oral health initiatives in Botswana. For her, dentistry is not limited to procedures. It is part of supporting long-term health.

Our Approach

At Lefkara Dental & Aesthetics, beauty is considered most meaningful when it reflects health. Each patient journey begins with a detailed consultation followed by treatment planning that respects safety and long-term outcomes.

Treatments are designed to enhance natural features rather than alter identity, allowing confidence to develop through balanced care.

Our Values

Evidence-Based

Clinical decisions are grounded in current evidence and guided by careful examination, not assumptions.

Transparency

Treatment is explained clearly at every stage so patients understand what is being recommended and why.

Informed Decision-Making

Patients are encouraged to participate actively in their own care and feel confident in the choices they make.

Calm & Considered

The environment is designed to feel reassuring and well organised from the first consultation onward.

A practice built around you

Whether you are attending for a routine dental visit or exploring aesthetic treatment for the first time, your care at Lefkara begins with listening. We take the time to understand what matters to you and build from there.
