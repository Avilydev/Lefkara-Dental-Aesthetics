Body Contouring & Slimming Treatments

Some areas of the body can change in ways that feel difficult to influence. You may notice persistent fullness or a gradual loss of firmness that does not improve despite consistent effort. At Lefkara Dental & Aesthetics, our dental practice in Phakalane approaches body contouring with careful evaluation first, assessing the treatment area and your expectations before recommending any non-surgical option, so that planning is guided by tissue response rather than assumption.

What treatment options are available?

Following consultation, treatment options may include:

  • Cavitation to target fat cells using ultrasound energy
  • Radiofrequency, including vacuum bipolar systems
  • Laser lipolysis for focused fat reduction
  • 6 in 1 slimming sessions using combined technology
  • BTL X Wave for areas affected by cellulite
  • Body sculpting wraps
  • RF skin tightening
  • Body microneedling for uneven texture
  • Body enzyme treatments

How do these treatments support contouring?

Cavitation, for example, uses ultrasound energy on specific areas, while radiofrequency gently warms deeper tissue layers to support tightening over time. Laser lipolysis directs low-level energy to localised fat deposits. Each approach is explained clearly during consultation before treatment begins.

What happens during a session?

During your appointment, the selected treatment is applied to the target area in a controlled setting. Most procedures allow you to return to normal activity shortly afterwards, although temporary warmth may be noticed.

Because skin quality influences contour results, some patients begin with skin analysis and facials so our team can evaluate tissue condition before recommending a treatment plan.

Is body contouring suitable for you?

If you are considering non-surgical shaping, consulting our dentist in Phakalane at Lefkara Dental & Aesthetics helps you determine whether these treatments align with your goals. An informed discussion ensures decisions are made with clarity.
