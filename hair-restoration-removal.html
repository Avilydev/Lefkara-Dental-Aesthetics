Hair Restoration & Hair Removal

Hair changes can feel unsettling. You may notice thinning in certain areas or increased shedding that was not present before, and over time, this can begin to affect how you feel about your appearance. At Lefkara Dental & Aesthetics, our dental practice in Phakalane begins by looking closely at your scalp and the pattern of hair thinning before suggesting any treatment.

How can hair restoration support regrowth?

Scalp condition and follicle activity are assessed carefully before recommending treatment. Depending on your findings, options may include:

  • Advanced Hair Complex to support follicle function
  • Green Peel hair treatment to stimulate circulation
  • Scalp microneedling to encourage regenerative response

These treatments aim to support the hair growth cycle gradually and require consistency over time.

What should you expect during treatment?

The scalp is prepared before treatment in a controlled environment. Some procedures may cause temporary sensitivity, which usually diminishes soon after. Our dentist will clarify session spacing and realistic progress expectations.

If scalp texture or underlying tissue quality requires additional stimulation, our dental team in Phakalane may also explore options within our skin analysis and facials treatments as part of a broader plan.

What about hair removal services?

Hair removal services are selected according to the treatment area and skin type. Options include alkaline wash treatments chosen according to skin response and treatment goals.

Is hair treatment right for you?

If shedding increases or unwanted hair persists, book an appointment with our dentist in Phakalane at Lefkara Dental & Aesthetics. Schedule a consultation to explore options for healthier scalp and hair management.
